Who We Are

iNesso was founded by JJ Olsson in Jacksonville, FL in order to support the educational needs of classrooms and parents seeking to explore VR. Our team is composed of experts in virtual reality, gaming, and a variety of other fields. Our mission is to support the educational needs of our community through virtual reality.
